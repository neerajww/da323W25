- [MNIST: Handwritten Digits (unimodal)](https://yann.lecun.com/exdb/mnist/)  
- [Fashion-MNIST: Zalando's Article Images (unimodal)](https://github.com/zalandoresearch/fashion-mnist)  
- [CIFAR-10/100: Tiny Natural Images Dataset (unimodal)](https://www.cs.toronto.edu/~kriz/cifar.html)  
- [CANDOR: Conversational Audio-Visual Dataset for Human Interaction (multimodal)](https://guscooney.com/candor-dataset/)  
- [Coswara: Breathing, Cough, and Speech Sounds for COVID-19 Diagnosis (unimodal - audio)](https://coswara.iisc.ac.in/)  
- [ImageNet: Large-Scale Visual Recognition Dataset (unimodal - image)](https://www.image-net.org/)  
- [WIT: Wikipedia-based Image-Text Dataset (multimodal)](https://github.com/google-research-datasets/wit)  
- [AudioSet: Audio Events in YouTube Videos (unimodal - audio)](https://research.google.com/audioset/)  
- [VoxCeleb: Large-Scale Speaker Recognition Dataset (multimodal - audio & video)](https://www.robots.ox.ac.uk/~vgg/data/voxceleb/)
